Extra elements

ZKIN comes with extra elements such as section title, dividers to help you building impressive websites and applications.

Section title

ZKIN comes with four title styles and horizontal alignment in section title style 2, 3, 4 which you can easily change them to center, left or right aligned. Also includes title color scheme for white background, dark background and color background.
